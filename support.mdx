---
title: "Support"
description: "Where to go for help and sharing feedback."
---

If you encounter any technical issues with Apitally, or have questions or feedback, please join the conversation in our [GitHub Discussions](https://github.com/orgs/apitally/discussions) or on [Twitter / X](https://twitter.com/apitally).

You can expect a prompt response from [Simon](https://twitter.com/simongurcke), the founder of Apitally, or the community.

For any non-technical matters, please [send us an email](mailto:hello@apitally.io) and we'll get back to you as soon as possible.
