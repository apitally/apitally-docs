---
title: "Quickstart"
description: "Get started in under 5 minutes."
---

1. Click the **Get started** button above to sign up or log in.
2. Select **Create app** in Apitally, enter a name for your app and select your framework.
3. Follow the setup instructions within Apitally to install the appropriate **client library** in your project and add the Apitally **middleware** to your application.
4. Deploy your application and see it come to life in Apitally!

For more detailed instructions select your framework below.
